Return Policy

We want you to love every piece you receive from Haya & Noor. If for any reason you're not completely satisfied, we're happy to accept returns within 30 days of the delivery date.

  • Items must be unworn, unwashed and in their original condition
  • All original tags must still be attached
  • Items must be returned in their original packaging
  • Free returns for all UK orders
  • International returns: £10 return shipping fee (deducted from refund)
⚠️ Please note: Limited edition and sale items are final sale and cannot be returned unless faulty.

Exchanges

Want a different size or colour? We'll make it easy. Simply request an exchange when you initiate your return, and we'll send out your new item as soon as we receive your return.

  • Exchanges are subject to availability
  • Size exchanges are free of charge
  • Exchange items are sent via standard delivery (3–5 business days)
  • If your preferred size is unavailable, a full refund will be issued

Faulty Items

In the rare event that you receive a faulty or damaged item, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the issue. We will arrange a free replacement or full refund as a priority.

📧 Email us at: hello@hayanoor.co.uk with your order number and a photo of the fault.

Refund Timeline

  • Return received and inspected: 1–2 business days
  • Refund approved and processed: 1–2 business days
  • Funds appear in your account: 3–5 business days (depends on your bank)
  • Total time from return receipt to refund: up to 10 business days

Still Have Questions?

Our customer care team is here to help with any returns or exchange queries.
