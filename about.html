Who We Are

Crafted with Purpose

Haya & Noor was founded on the belief that modest fashion should never mean compromising on style. We create pieces that celebrate the modern Muslim woman — confident, elegant, and unapologetically herself.

Every abaya in our collection is thoughtfully designed with premium fabrics, refined silhouettes, and meticulous attention to detail. From everyday essentials to occasion wear, we craft for every chapter of your life.

Our name says it all — Haya, meaning modesty, and Noor, meaning light. Together, we believe in clothing that radiates both.

Our Journey

How We Got Here

2021

The Idea is Born

Frustrated by a lack of stylish, high-quality modest wear, two sisters begin sketching their dream abaya line from a London bedroom.

2022

First Collection Launches

Haya & Noor officially launches with 6 designs. The first drop sells out within 72 hours. The community responds with overwhelming love.

2023

Growing the Family

We expand to accessories, launch our signature Dubai Kaftan line, and partner with artisan embroiderers for our occasion collection.

2024

International Reach

Orders arrive from over 25 countries. We are featured in Vogue Arabia and Modest Fashion Week London. The dream is becoming real.

2025

The New Chapter

A flagship London pop-up, a rebrand, and our most beautiful collection yet. Haya & Noor steps into its next era.

2026

Today & Beyond

A full e-commerce relaunch, new seasonal drops, and a commitment to sustainable luxury modest fashion for every woman.
