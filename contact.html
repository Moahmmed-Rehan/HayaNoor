Quick Answers

Frequently Asked

What sizes do you offer?

We offer sizes XS to 3XL across all collections. Our sizing guide page has full measurements to help you find the perfect fit.

How long does delivery take?

UK standard delivery is 3–5 business days. Express next-day delivery is available. International orders typically arrive in 7–14 days.

Can I return or exchange items?

Yes — we offer free returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original packaging. See our Returns page for full details.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to over 40 countries. International shipping costs are calculated at checkout. Free international shipping on orders over £200.

How do I track my order?

You'll receive an email with your tracking link as soon as your order ships. You can also log in to your account to check order status.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes — complimentary gift wrapping is available on all orders. Just select the option at checkout and we'll take care of it beautifully.
