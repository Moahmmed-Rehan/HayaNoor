Sizing Guide

Every abaya is designed to be fluid and graceful. Use this guide to find the fit that feels just right for you.

Home/Sizing Guide

How to Measure

📏

Chest / Bust

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape measure parallel to the ground.

⭕

Waist

Measure around your natural waistline, which is the narrowest part of your torso.

📐

Hips

Measure around the fullest part of your hips, approximately 20cm below your waistline.

↕

Length / Height

Stand straight and measure from the top of your shoulder to where you'd like the abaya to fall.

💪

Sleeve Length

Measure from the shoulder seam down to your wrist bone with your arm slightly bent.

✦

Pro Tip

Always measure over light clothing or lingerie. Have someone help you for the most accurate measurements.

Styling Tips

For taller women (5'8"+), consider our Long Length option available on selected styles — email us to request.

Open abayas can be worn open over outfits or fastened with a belt for a structured look.

If in doubt between two sizes, always size up — our abayas are designed to be worn modestly and comfortably.

Kaftans are one-size-fits-a-range — if your measurements fall between two, the kaftan will still drape beautifully.

All fabrics are pre-washed and tested for minimal shrinkage. Wash in cold water to preserve colour and shape.

Not sure? Email us at hello@hayanoor.co.uk with your measurements and we'll personally recommend your perfect size.
