Order Processing

All orders placed before 1pm Monday–Friday are dispatched the same day. Orders placed after 1pm or on weekends will be dispatched the next business day.

During busy periods (Eid, sale events, new drops), processing times may extend by 1–2 additional business days. We'll always keep you updated via email.
